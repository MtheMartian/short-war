Card Game: War (Short Ver.)

Instructions:

Click "DRAW" to draw cards (Will draw for both players)

Game Rules

Players draw a card each, card with the highest rank wins and both cards are added the winner's pile. If the cards drawn have the same rank "War" is triggered. During "War", 3 cards are automatically drawn face down for both players and the player who wins the next draw takes all of the cards.

PLAYER WITH MOST CARDS WINS!
